New technology

These days new versions of songs went viral that, listened to with headphones, sound very different from the original. Haven't you heard yet? Surprise yourself

What is it about?

It is a technique that emerged in the early 70s in England, which served a function of giving the sound a spherical characteristic.

Broadly speaking, from a complicated work that has a mathematical basis, the phase of the sound is worked on that allows the brain to distinguish where the information comes from.

Current jobs

Music Lab is currently working on an album entirely in 360 ° sound with Argentine and foreign artists such as Proyecto Gómez Casa, Lula Bertoldi, Loli Molina, Chancha Vía Circuito, Darío Jalfin, Nahuel Bronzini and Famasloop.

Today he works not only on a musical level but also on sound, innovating in albums but also in sound installations at conferences and presentations.
